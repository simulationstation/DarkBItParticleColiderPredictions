## Model Definition

The cross section per channel α is modeled as:

```
σ_α(√s) = norm_α × |BW₁(s) + R_α×BW₂(s) + C3_α×BW₃(s)|² + B_α(√s)
```

Where:
- `BWₖ(s) = Mₖ×Γₖ / (s - Mₖ² + i×Mₖ×Γₖ)` (relativistic Breit-Wigner)
- `R_α = r_α × exp(i×φ_α)` is the shared-subspace ratio (Y₂/Y₁)
- `C3_α` is the channel-specific third resonance amplitude
- `B_α(√s) = b0_α + b1_α×(√s - E₀) + b2_α×(√s - E₀)²` is additive background

1. **Fixed resonance shapes**: Masses and widths were fixed to PDG-like values.

2. **Preliminary data**: Using published cross-sections, not official BESIII
   covariance matrices. Correlated systematics not accounted for.

   approximate. The fit absorbs extra structure but doesn't precisely identify it.
